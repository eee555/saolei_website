# Golden Sheep Cup

The Golden Sheep Cup is a long-running tournament hosted by [Guo Jinyang](https://openms.top/#/player/48). It is held every April and October and lasts for 6 days.

## Scoring Rules

The Golden Sheep Cup counts replays uploaded during the tournament.

The following replays are used:

- Beginner: the best 20 games, requiring `3BV >= 10`
- Intermediate: the best 12 games, requiring `3BV >= 30`
- Expert: the best 5 games, requiring `3BV >= 100`

If a player does not have enough games, default times are used to fill the missing slots:

| Level | Default time |
| --- | --- |
| Beginner | 10 seconds |
| Intermediate | 60 seconds |
| Expert | 240 seconds |

Final rankings are based on the total time of these replays. Lower total time ranks higher.

## Accepted Software

### MetaSweeper

You can join directly by using MetaSweeper's tournament identifier feature.

### Minesweeper Arbiter

During the tournament, you need to register an Arbiter tournament identifier on the tournament detail page.

The identifier must end with the tournament identifier shown on the tournament page. For example, if the page gives `G12345`, then your Arbiter identifier must contain that ending.

After registration succeeds, replays uploaded with that Arbiter identifier will be recognized as tournament replays.
