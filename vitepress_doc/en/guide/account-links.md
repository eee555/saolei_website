# Account Links

Account links connect your identity on different platforms to your Open Minesweeper account. You can use this feature to help other players recognize you across communities. The feature is available in the **Account Links** tab on your profile page.

You need to sign in before you can manage your own account links.

## Linking Process

1. On the account links page, if you have not linked every supported platform, you will see a plus button. Click it to open a dialog that guides you through the linking process.
2. After entering the platform and id, you need a moderator to verify the account so that nobody can claim someone else's account.

### About ids

The word "id" can be ambiguous. On some websites it may be confused with a username or nickname. For all platforms currently supported by Open Minesweeper, the id should be a string of digits. After you choose a platform, the page should show instructions for finding that platform's id.

Once confirmed, the link cannot currently be undone directly, so please enter the id carefully. If you entered a wrong id, contact a moderator.

### Let a moderator know you
The moderators can see your link request. If they already know you, they will approve it directly. If they do not know you, you need to contact them for approval.

Saolei.wang, Minesweeper.Online, Bilibili, and Tencent QQ all support private messages. You can directly message a moderator on those platforms and tell them your Open Minesweeper id. If the information matches, the moderator should verify the account.

Authoritative Minesweeper lets you link your Saolei.wang and Minesweeper.Online accounts. If those accounts match the accounts linked on Open Minesweeper, a moderator should verify your Authoritative Minesweeper account.

## Privacy

Open Minesweeper uses real names. Once you link an account from another platform, other users on that platform may be able to learn your real name. Some account links are used only for customer support, and those accounts are private and visible only to moderators. Tencent QQ is currently a private platform.

## FAQ

### Why does it say "account not linked"?

To prevent account impersonation, all account links are manually reviewed by moderators. You need to contact a moderator, usually through the group chat. If the moderator knows you and can confirm that you own the account, they will approve it directly. If they cannot confirm your identity, you also need to contact the moderator's account on the corresponding platform. You can find that information in the moderator's **Account Links** tab.

### I entered the wrong id. What should I do?

The website currently does not support direct id editing. Please contact a moderator.

### Why are creator platforms not private?

We plan to develop a creator feature that lets you promote your minesweeper contents on Open Minesweeper. Once you start promoting your work, your account on that creator platform is already public. If you do not need to promote your work, there is no need to link a creator account, and we do not recommend doing so.

### How do I remove an account link?

The website currently does not support removing account links directly. Please contact a moderator.
